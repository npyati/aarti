Appointment Info

Initial Consultation

An initial no-fee phone consultation allows for us to consider clinical fit. I strongly believe in the therapeutic connection, which can begin in the first conversation.

Feel free to inquire about how I work and what I value in the therapeutic process. I will do my best to give as clear a sense as I can of what you might expect when working with me. I encourage you to assess whether my approach and style could be a good fit for you.

If you have been in therapy before, you may find it helpful to consider:

  • What has worked for you in the past
  • A particular therapeutic approach you are interested in at this time
  • What you are looking for in a therapist

If you have not been in therapy before, you may find it helpful to consider:

  • What concerns you may have about starting this process
  • Questions you may have about what to expect
  • What brings you to try therapy now

Payment

I use Ivy Pay to accept payment; it works with credit, debit, HSA and FSA cards. It is HIPAA-secure, keeps therapy information confidential, and facilitates the payment process. Your mobile number is entered, after which you will then receive a text message with a prompt to enter your payment information.

Insurance

Though I am not affiliated with insurance companies, upon request I can provide you with monthly billing summaries that you can submit to your insurance provider for reimbursement.

Cancellation and Late Policies

24-Hour Notice

Please provide 24-hour advance notice for cancellation to avoid fee for session.

Missed Appointments

Missed appointments and appointments cancelled without 24-hour notice are charged full fee.

Late Arrivals

If you will be arriving late, please contact me to let me know. Note that the session will still end at the regularly scheduled time.
