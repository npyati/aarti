Supporting you through life's transitions

I am a licensed clinical psychologist in private practice located in Beachwood, OH. I provide short-term and long-term therapy for individuals, couples, and groups. I specialize in support through life transitions and identity development.

Schedule a Consultation

I work with concerns related to:

  • Underlying anxious and/or depressed mood
  • Relationship building
  • Belonging and marginality
  • Relationship separation
  • Grief and loss
  • Identity formation and development from adolescence into adulthood
  • Self-worth and empowerment

Therapy Options

Individual Therapy

A customized approach to what you are experiencing, personalized to your background and needs.

Couples Therapy

Working towards a better relationship, identifying whether a relationship is viable, and/or working through dissolution.

Group Therapy

One of the best ways to take the work done in individual therapy to the next level.
